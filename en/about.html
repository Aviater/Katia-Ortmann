My name is Katia Ortmann. I have been active in the interior decorating sector for more than 25 years now. What all started as a passionate hobby has grown into my full-time job. After having worked in several interior design stores in Marbella and having decorated showhouses on Fuerteventura, I started working on my own after 20 years thanks to various requests from customers.

The customer is the first and most importand priority for me. This starts by looking together for the appropriate design, design phase and delivery of the project. Every customer has his personal taste. That's why we're looking for a unique interior in which you feel at home and with a budget-friendly result.
Whether it's a closet, sofa, bed linen or a complete interior, my goal is a satisfied customer.
